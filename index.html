Welcome to My Digital Corner

Hi there! I’m Sepgod, a digital culture enthusiast with a passion for stories that transcend the screen. This space was born from a simple need: to document and share my fascination with the worlds we inhabit—whether through a controller, a line of code, or an animated series.

> What Will You Find Here?

My goal is to explore the intersection of nostalgia and innovation. On this blog, we dive deep into:

  • Retro Gaming: From the magic of 8 and 16-bit eras to the hunt for classic hardware. We celebrate the art of collecting and the titles that defined entire genres.
  • Anime and Japan: Analysis of impactful stories—ranging from gritty cyberpunk to laid-back slice-of-life—alongside my journey learning the language and exploring the culture.
  • Tech and PC Gaming: Guides on components, building powerful rigs, and how modern technology can elevate our favorite hobbies.
  • Personal Projects: A look behind the scenes of my own experiments, from creating pixel art to managing small-scale digital projects.

> My Philosophy

I firmly believe that hobbies aren't just a way to pass the time; they are a way to understand the world and connect with others. Whether I’m analyzing the economy of an RPG or hunting for the perfect aesthetic on a vintage digital camera, this blog is for those who see details where others only see pixels.
